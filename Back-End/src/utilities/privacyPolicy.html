Privacy Policy of beComfree

At beComfree, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect when you use our website or make purchases through our ecommerce platform, how we use and protect that information, and your rights regarding your personal data.

Information We Collect

When you interact with our platform, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: This may include your name, email address, postal address, and phone number when you register on our platform or make a purchase.
  • Payment Information: When you make a purchase, we collect the necessary information to process your payment, such as credit card details, billing address, and other payment-related information.
  • Browsing Information: We automatically collect data about how you interact with our website, including the products you view, the pages you visit, and the duration of your visit. This may include information such as your IP address, browser type, device used, and approximate geographical location.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance the user experience, personalize content and ads, and analyze the performance of our website. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings.

How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Processing Orders: To process and fulfill your orders, including communicating with you about your order status.
  • Improving Our Services: To analyze trends and user interactions in order to improve the functionality and performance of our website and ecommerce platform.
  • Marketing Communications: With your consent, to send you promotional emails about new products, special offers, and other updates. You can opt out of these communications at any time.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations, such as tax and accounting requirements, or to respond to lawful requests from government authorities.
